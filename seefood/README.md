# Seefood 🌭

A hand-tracking game inspired by Silicon Valley's "SeeFood" app. Use your hands to sort images into "Hot Dog" or "Not Hot Dog" categories

[Video](https://youtu.be/JRygJhzT1Hw) | [Live Demo](https://www.funwithcomputervision.com/seefood/)

## What It Does

- **Hand Tracking**: Uses your webcam to track your hands in real-time
- **Gesture Controls**: 
  - First hand controls a rotating circle of images (pinch to resize)
  - Second hand drags images to sorting boxes
- **Visual Effects**: Military-style radar HUD and particle effects
- **Game Logic**: Sort all images correctly to complete the mission

## How to Play

1. Allow webcam access when prompted
2. Hold up your first hand and pinch thumb/index finger to control the image circle
3. Use your second hand to pinch and drag images from the circle
4. Drop images into the correct boxes:
   - **Green box**: Hot Dog
   - **Red box**: Not Hot Dog
5. Complete the mission by sorting all images!

## Tech Stack

- **Three.js**: 3D graphics and rendering
- **MediaPipe**: Hand landmark detection
- **WebRTC**: Webcam access
- **Vanilla JavaScript**: Game logic and interactions
