Gesture-based 3D Playground
Interactive demo: control 3D shapes with hand gestures
How to play
– Show your hand to the camera
– Pinch fingers to grab a shape
– Move hand to move the shape
– Use two hands to create & scale
Status: ready
Bring hands close and pinch to create a shape
